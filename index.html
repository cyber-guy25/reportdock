Your one-stop solution for creating KPI dashboards and comprehensive reports.

Empower Your Data

Transform raw data into actionable insights with our powerful dashboard and reporting tools. Whether you're an individual, a company, or an MSP, Report Dock provides the flexibility and features you need to succeed.

Features

  • Secure User Registration and Login

    Ensure your data is protected with our robust authentication system. Register easily and choose from various account types to suit your needs.

  • Role-Based Access Control

    Manage access to features and data with precision. Our role-based system ensures that users only see what they need to.

  • Integration with Stripe for Subscriptions

    Seamlessly manage your subscriptions with Stripe integration. Enjoy hassle-free payment processing and tier management.

  • Custom API Integration Management

    Connect to various data sources with ease. Our platform supports both prebuilt and custom API integrations, allowing you to tailor your data ecosystem.

  • Custom Card Creation for Data Visualization

    Create custom cards to visualize your data in the way that makes the most sense for you. Choose from text, graphs, charts, and more.

  • Dashboard Display with Automatic Refresh

    Keep your data up-to-date with automatic refreshes. Arrange your custom cards on a dashboard for a comprehensive view of your key metrics.

  • Custom Report Generation and Sharing

    Design and share professional reports with ease. Use our WYSIWYG editor to create whitelabelled reports and export them as PDFs.
